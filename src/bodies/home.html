01 — The thesis

A merchant bank for the coconut industry.

Most exporters compete on price and absorb the risk. We do the opposite: we hold the chain and the capital, so the buyer holds certainty. Asset-light, evidence-led — we profit from risk management and credit standing, not from cutting margins.
