Traceability you can audit.

For us sustainability is evidence, not adjectives: traceability to the farmer, primate-free sourcing and smallholder financing.

Three commitments, each checkable.

Traceability to the farmer

Each lot ties back to a sourcing zone and the households within it — an audit trail, not a claim.

Primate-free sourcing

No monkey-picked coconut in our chain — a documented condition of supply.

Smallholder financing

Capital reaches growers, not just buyers — stabilising supply where it starts.
