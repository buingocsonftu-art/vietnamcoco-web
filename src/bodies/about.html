About — Định giá lại dừa Việt

We revalue Vietnamese coconut.

An asset-light merchant bank and trading house for the coconut industry — built to deliver certainty of price and supply, grove to factory.

01 — The model

Merchant bank, not middleman.

Commodity traders compete on price and pass volatility down the chain. We compete on certainty. We hold the chain and the capital, so the risk sits with us and the predictability sits with the buyer.

The model is asset-light: we earn from risk management and credit standing, not from owning every truck and tank. That keeps us fast, accountable, and bankable.

What we are

A trade-finance house that holds the chain and the capital behind a contract.

What we are not

A broker reselling other people's coconut on the lowest spot price of the week.
