Most coconut exporters compete on price and absorb the volatility themselves. The buyers we meet in the EU and US are not paying for a lower per-tonne number — they pay to make price and supply stop moving.

That is the thesis behind VIETNAMCOCO: hold the chain and the capital so the buyer holds certainty. Trade finance and grove-to-factory control let us underwrite a contracted price and a contracted volume, instead of passing market swings down the line.
