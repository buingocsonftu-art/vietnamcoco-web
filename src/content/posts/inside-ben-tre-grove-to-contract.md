Supply-zone locking starts with a handshake and a GPS pin.

In Bến Tre, a grove becomes a contract long before the first nut is cut. We map the trees, agree a price band for the season, and register the plot — so the volume a buyer signs for is the volume that ships.

## Why it matters

A bankable contract needs a known origin. By controlling the chain from the grove, we can stand behind both quality and quantity, instead of buying anonymous blended lots on the spot market.
